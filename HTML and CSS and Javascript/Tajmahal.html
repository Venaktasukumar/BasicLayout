TAJ MAHAL

History of Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a white marble mausoleum located in Agra, India. It was built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. Construction began in 1632 and was completed in 1653. The Taj Mahal is considered to be one of the greatest examples of Mughal architecture and a symbol of love.

Visiting Taj Mahal

  • Location: Agra, India
  • Visiting hours: Sunrise to sunset, closed on Fridays
  • Entry fee: INR 50 for Indian nationals, INR 1100 for foreign nationals
